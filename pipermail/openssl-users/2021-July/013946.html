> https://openssl-cng-engine.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html .
> 
> The project implements the majority of the EVP interface, to leverage the BCrypt crypto
> implementations, as well as a subset of the STORE interface, for integration with the
> Windows Certificate and Keystore(s), via the NCrypt and Cert APIs. It has been tested with 1.1.1k
> on Windows 10, with Visual Studio 2017 and 2019. It is released under the Apache-2.0 license.
