> In Openssl 1.1.1a, how can I force the TLS1.2 user to use the 
> "rsa_pkcs_sha512" signature algorithm? Many thanks in advance.

openssl s_client -sigalgs RSA+SHA512
or 
SSL_CTX_set1_sigalgs_list(ctx, "RSA+SHA512");
