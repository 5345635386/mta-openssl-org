Hi Viktor,

> By "a file" you clearly mean a "PEM file" with one or more certificates
exclosed in "-----BEGIN ...".."-----END ..." delimiters.

Yes, this is what I mean.

> openssl crl2pkcs7 -nocrl -certfile somefile.pem |
        opessl pkcs7 -print_certs -text
