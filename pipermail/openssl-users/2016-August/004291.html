On 08/26/16 18:51, Matt Caswell wrote:
>
> DSA needs parameters specifying.
>
> Try this:
>
> openssl genpkey -genparam -algorithm DSA -pkeyopt dsa_paramgen_bits:2048
> -out dsa.params
>
> openssl genpkey -paramfile dsa.params -out dsa.key
>
>
> Matt
