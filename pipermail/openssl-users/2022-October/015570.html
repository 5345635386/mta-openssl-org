Hello Everyone,

I have written a new book about OpenSSL, the title is "Demystifying
Cryptography with OpenSSL 3.0". The book was published yesterday.

The book can be useful for many subscribers of this mailing list, including
software developers, system and network administrators, DevOps specialists,
and everyone who wants to learn more about cryptography, certificates, and
network security. In this book, I am trying to explain complex technologies
in simple words and giving practical OpenSSL-powered command-line and code
examples to illustrate the explanations. May your applications and networks
always be secure! :)

You can check out the book here:
https://www.amazon.com/Demystifying-Cryptography-OpenSSL-3-0-techniques/dp/1800560346/

Med vennlig hilsen / Best regards,
Alexei.
