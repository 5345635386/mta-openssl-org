  *   When I see SSL certificates, their serial number is like this : 0A:8D:9A:4Q:8X:1A:0B:88:18:1Z
Serial numbers are displayed as hex values, so my guess is you just typed that “as an example” since Q X Z aren’t valid hex characters.  Or perhaps it was an extension (like device serial) that isn’t the “real” serial number.

On master, you can specify a random serial number
