First and foremost, we would like to express our heartfelt appreciation 
for your overwhelming interest in the upcoming Providers Meeting(*). 
The volume of enthusiastic responses far exceeded our expectations.

In light of the large number of interested participants, we've decided 
to ensure we create an event experience that is informative and 
organized in the best possible manner for all involved. To achieve 
this, we believe it is essential to allocate more time to plan and 
structure the meeting.

Given our current commitments and the impending v3.2 release, we are 
targeting the months of November-December for the Providers Meeting. We 
are confident that this timeline will allow us to prepare a program 
that addresses key topics.

In the meantime, we encourage everyone to share their thoughts, 
questions, and suggestions. Just send an email to feedback at openssl.org

Thank you for your understanding, patience, and continued enthusiasm.

Anton
