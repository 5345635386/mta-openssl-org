that in addition to IAIK that Bouncy Castle had similar issues. We are also aware of
customers that will be impacted by the upcoming releases if certificates with fractional
time fails to verify.

I think it's important to maintain interoperability even in the event that there are
minor profile violations.  If there is anything I can do to help move this forward
please let me know.
