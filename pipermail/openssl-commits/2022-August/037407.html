Hello!

The openssl organization has been removed from the OpenSSL Software Services  enterprise.

The following changes have been made:

- Billing, identity management, 2FA requirements, and other policies are no longer governed by the OpenSSL Software Services  enterprise.
- The organization has been downgraded to the free plan.
- The organization is now governed by our standard Terms of Service.
- Any internal repositories within the organization have been converted to private repositories.

If this was unexpected, please confirm with other owners of the enterprise or organization, or contact GitHub Support at https://github.com/contact.

Thanks,
The GitHub Team
