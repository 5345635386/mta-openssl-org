-Dear donors, we have figured out a way for you to donate to OpenSSL through your UnionPay card. This can be done
-by opening an account with <a href="https://www.paypal.com/c2/webapps/mpp/get-started?locale.x=zh_CN">Paypal China</a>
- and associating
-your UnionPay card with this Paypal account. This is the most convenient method we have been able to find for Chinese donors.
-<p>
-Instruction 1
-<br>
-For those who have no Paypal China account: 
-<br>
-1. Please open an account on <a href="https://www.paypal.com/c2/webapps/mpp/get-started?locale.x=zh_CN">PayPal China</a>, and associate your UnionPay card with this account. PayPal China <a href="https://www.paypal.com/c2/webapps/mpp/faqs-china-unionpay-payments?locale.x=zh_C2">has instructions</a> on how make that association.
-<br>
-2. Once you are signed in on Paypal China, click the "send money” button on the MyPaypal page. Please choose the US dollar as
-the currency. Paypal China will pay in US dollars but charge you in RMB based on the current exchange rate. There is no need
-to go to a bank for the currency exchange. 
-then fill in our email address <em>paypal at opensslfoundation.org</em> as the receiver, then follow the instructions of Paypal
-itself to complete the donation.
-<br>
-3. After you have an account with Paypal China, you can also go to the OpenSSL <a href="donations.html">Donations</a> web page,
-and follow our “Instruction 2” (below) to complete your donation.
-<p>
-Instruction 2
-<br>
-For those who already have a Paypal China account:
-<br>
-1. Click the yellow “Donate” button above.
-<br>
-2. Go into the donation page and fill in the amount you want to donate.
-Please choose the US dollar as
-the currency, and log in to Paypal China through this page. Paypal China will pay in US dollars but
-charge you in RMB based on the current exchange rate. There is no need to go to a bank for the currency exchange.
-<br>
-3. After you have logged in to Paypal China, click the “donate” button at the bottom to complete your donation.
-<p>
-If you have any questions please send an email to <a href="mailto:fangxie at opensslfoundation.org">fangxie at opensslfoundation.org</a>. We will reply as soon as possible.
